About Me

My Story

Hi I'm Sam Angelos, I'm a UI designer based in Boise, Idaho. I'm a astute hard working individual, who offers a great sense of design practices and abilities I collaborate well with teams and other designers to accomplish whatever creative vision is needed for a particular project. I bring a track record of innovation, quality, and a unique creative vision. My passion for design fuels a continuous pursuit of improvement, making me an asset to any team or project.

I have been practicing UI design for about a year and a half now, and have just recently completed the UI design course at CareerFoundry. I have been immersed in design from an early age, experimenting with various artistic mediums. Despite a lifelong passion for design, I struggled to find a proffesional outlet that truly resonated. It wasn't until stumbling upon the world of user interface design that everything fell into place.
